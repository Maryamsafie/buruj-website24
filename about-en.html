About Buroj

Buroj Food Industries was established in 2018 as one of the leading Libyan companies in the field of juices, beverages, cakes and various food products.


Since its launch, Buroj has focused on providing high–quality products at competitive prices, while committing to food safety standards and building long–term trust with consumers in the local market, with ambitions to expand to regional markets.

Our Vision


To make Buroj a leading brand in the juice and food market, representing quality, innovation and reliability, and able to compete locally and regionally with strong products and a clear brand identity.

Our Mission


To offer safe and diverse food products that satisfy the taste of the Libyan consumer, manufactured using modern food safety and quality systems, with continuous development in taste, appearance and packaging to meet market requirements.

Our Values


  • Quality in every stage of production.
  • Commitment to food safety standards.
  • Building long–term trust with consumers and partners.
  • Continuous innovation in products and packaging.

Strengths & Advantages


  • Clear vision & strong identity: well–studied growth strategy and a distinctive brand.
  • Advanced production lines: modern equipment and continuous quality control.
  • Diverse product range: juices and beverages in different sizes to serve individuals and families.
  • Quality & safety: applying food safety systems and strict control standards.
  • Expansion plans: working to expand the distribution network inside Libya and reach new markets abroad.
  • Effective management: qualified administrative and technical teams working to maintain and improve product quality.
